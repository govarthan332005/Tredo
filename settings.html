❓ Help Center

How do I place an order?

Browse writings on home page, tap one you like, fill in your requirements, and tap "Place Order".

How does pickup work?

Our delivery partner will come to your location to pick up your book/script at the scheduled time.

Can I cancel an order?

Yes, you can cancel orders before pickup. Open order details and tap "Cancel Order".

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI, and Credit/Debit Cards.
